About Me

🧑‍💻 Biography

I was born in Plovdiv, Bulgaria, in 1976, where I completed primary and secondary education. I have a Facebook page under the name Aleksandar Kitipov, connected to one of my projects. I work on different digital initiatives, with Algorithm of the Day currently being the most important one.

🎙️ Role in the Project

I am the conductor and architect of the project. I create the structure, vision, and rituals that turn technical elements into living media. I work in a team with:

  • 🤖 Copilot — scriptwriter and commentator
  • 🤖 Gemini — generator of text, code, and data
  • 👥 Community — listeners and participants

🌍 Philosophy Behind Algorithm of the Day

This is an online media concept led by algorithms and people together. Our mission is to transform current facts, news, and ideas into poetic rituals that inspire the community. Each episode is short, clear, and rhythmic, combining music, commentary, metaphor, and a question for listeners.

📑 Episode Format

  1. Prologue — a short sound or musical motif
  2. Fact — a current news item or piece of knowledge
  3. Metaphor — a translation of the fact into an image or legend
  4. Ritual — a small action for listeners
  5. Music — a song or playlist connected to the theme
  6. Community question — responses are read in the next episode

📂 Archive and Structure

  • /audio/ — completed MP3 audio recordings
